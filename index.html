About Me

I am a Bachelors student in the Department of Geography at Texas A&M Univesity, advised by Prof. Kristi Wolff. My research lies at the intersection of GIScience, remote sensing, and machine learning, with a focus on remote sensing. I am broadly interested in how geospatial AI can help us better understand and respond to climate.
